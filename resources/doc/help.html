♦ Pentachoron ♦
– Four dimensional rotation visualizer –
♦

This application visualize the rotation of the simplest four dimensional object, the pentachoron. It could rotate the object around the six basic planes with simple-, double- and isoclinic-rotation types.

This application was inspired by the book Epoch.

♦ Basic User Guide ♦

It is possible to rotate the basic coordinate system by pressing, holding and moving touch-point / mouse on 3D-view. Double clicking on 3D-view will reset the viewpoint.

To rotate pentachoron around one or two planes you just have to rotate the dials at bottom side. You can select the plane of rotation at above the dials. The default rotation plane is „xy” plane. Click on option box, choose different planes and see what will happen. If you want to start an automatic animation just click „Animate” checkbox.

With „Double” checkbox you can setup a four dimensional rotation around two planes.

With „Isoclinic” checkbox you can setup a four dimensional isoclinic rotation (it is a special case of double rotation when the rotation angle around two planes are equal).

If you would like to rotate pentachoron around more than two planes, then click on the „Full control” tab and tweak the dials.

„Special rotation” tab contains a combined rotation control, it tries to give back the phases of rotation given in book Epoch”. It actually rotates the pentachoron around XY, YZ, and YW planes at once ( Step1: XZ=-30°, YZ=60°; Step2: XZ=-60°; Step3: XZ=-30°, YW=-60°).

Double clicking on the middle of any dial (where it displays the angle of rotation) always resets that angle value.
